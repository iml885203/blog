指定 TimeMachine 備份時間

[https://tclementdev.com/timemachineeditor/](https://tclementdev.com/timemachineeditor/)

![](/images/posts/macos-personal-productivity-software/time-machine-editor.png)

## AirBuddy

> 需付費，$9.99 鎂

AirPods 進階管理工具，快捷鍵切換降噪/通透非常方便

[https://v2.airbuddy.app/](https://v2.airbuddy.app/)

![](/images/posts/macos-personal-productivity-software/air-buddy.png)

## BetterTouchTool

> 需付費，$10 鎂(2年內可更新)、 $22 鎂(終生)

自定義快捷鍵工具，可搭配 AirBuddy 實現切換 `降噪功能` 與 `音樂` 同時開關

[https://folivora.ai/](https://folivora.ai/)

![](/images/posts/macos-personal-productivity-software/better-touch-tool.png)

## Middle

> 需付費，約 $10 鎂

[https://middleclick.app/](https://middleclick.app/)

![](/images/posts/macos-personal-productivity-software/middle.png)

## SteerMouse

> 需付費， $19.99 鎂

修正非原廠滑鼠滾動速度

[https://plentycom.jp/en/steermouse/](https://plentycom.jp/en/steermouse/)
